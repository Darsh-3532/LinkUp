Evidence-Based Ecosystem Recommendations

Actionable guidance for ecosystem builders, investors, and policymakers based on comprehensive network analysis and proven success patterns.

For Ecosystem Builders

Accelerators, Government, Economic Development

1. Focus on Network Density

Prioritize programs that increase connections between ecosystem participants rather than just adding new players. Our data shows density matters more than size.

2. Create Cross-Sector Events

Organize regular events that bring together different industries. Cross-sector collaborations show 43% higher success rates.

3. Measure Network Health

Track centrality measures and network density as key performance indicators alongside traditional metrics like jobs created or funding raised.

4. Support Bridge Builders

Identify and support individuals who naturally connect different parts of the ecosystem. These "bridge builders" are crucial for network health.

For Investors

VCs, Angels, Investment Analysts

1. Evaluate Network Position

Assess a startup's network centrality and connections to key ecosystem players as part of due diligence. Network position predicts success.

2. Invest in Network Building

Provide portfolio companies with introductions and networking opportunities. Your network becomes their competitive advantage.

3. Diversify Network Exposure

Actively seek opportunities in underrepresented segments. Our data shows significant untapped potential in female-founded startups.

4. Regional Expansion Strategy

Consider emerging ecosystems with growing network density. Early investment in developing regions offers significant upside potential.
